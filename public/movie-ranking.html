My all-time favourite movies in Haneefah's opinion

The top three on the list

Aquaman

What can I say about this movie. Imagine all the generation's film has degraded and suddenly a beacon comes down shining in the most boring hours of the day. that is how I feel when I think about this movie.

Jumanji

To all fans of adventure and mystery. this movie gives the satisfaction we crave for. For first time watchers, I recommend you get a popcon and a chilled soft drink, turn off the lights and give yourself one of the best movie nights.

The kid who will be king

I'm was just so happy that legends like King Artur, Morgana and Co could be brought back to reference such an amazing movie. This is certainly a must watch.
